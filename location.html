Location

The school is located in the Baguiati Area of North Kolkata. It is easily approachable from VIP Road, one of the main arterial roads connecting DumDum Airport with Kolkata.

Getting off at Baguiati Junction walk five minutes due East and then turn right to reach Calcutta Public School.
